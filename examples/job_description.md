Title: Staff Software Engineer, Platform
Company: Acme Cloud
Location: Remote (US)
Industry: B2B SaaS / Cloud Infrastructure

---

## About the Role

We're looking for a Staff Engineer to join our Platform team. You'll own the architecture
and technical direction of our core data ingestion and processing infrastructure — the
backbone of everything Acme Cloud does. We process over 50 billion events per month and
reliability and performance are table stakes.

This isn't a heads-down coding role. You'll spend roughly 40% of your time writing code,
40% on design, architecture, and review, and 20% on mentorship and cross-functional
collaboration.

## What You'll Do
- Own technical strategy and roadmap for our event ingestion, processing, and storage platform
- Lead a team of 6 engineers (two senior, four mid-level); set technical standards
- Partner with the infrastructure team on reliability and cost targets
- Collaborate with product and data science on platform capabilities
- Be the primary technical reviewer for all platform changes

## What We're Looking For

**Required:**
- 7+ years of software engineering experience, including 2+ years in a senior or staff role
- Deep expertise in Python and distributed systems
- Experience designing systems at scale (10M+ events/day or equivalent)
- Strong track record of technical leadership and mentoring

**Preferred:**
- Experience with Kafka or similar streaming infrastructure
- Familiarity with Kubernetes and AWS
- Demonstrated ability to reduce infrastructure costs through engineering

## Why Acme Cloud
- Fully remote, async-first culture
- Competitive compensation ($210K–$260K base + equity)
